A Hero's Journey

This is the story of Brett Bryant

Chapter 1

The Military life

Brett joined the U.S. Navy right out of high school. He served honorably for 4737 days, 155 months and 20 days, 12 years, 11 months and 20 days. He joined because he really had no where else to go. What seemed like a forced decision was really the first step in him discovering who he was. At first he thought, I am only doing four years but as time flew by, things got easier. He decided to reenlist. Never did he think this would be his career choice. C'est la vie. Eventually, time flew by and it reached a point where he would have to leave the only job he's ever known. That's okay because his next journey was one of self discovery and travel!

Chapter 2

The Bus Life!

Knowing he did not want to just jump back into some job, he and his partner decided to travel. They bought Bussy! Bussy was a 9 window Thomas Built Bus. They converted this tiny bus into a tiny home and travelled the United States. They left on Halloween and headed south. They traversed the the great land of their birth. They went to the Grand Canyon, drove up the California Coast, met interesting people and ate great food! But Alas! for it was not meant to be forever. Sadly, Bussy broke down. We enter another transition point in our hero's story...

Chapter 3

Enter Aniexty and the Fart Smelling town!

After Bussy broke down, there weren't many options for our heros. He and his partner decided to move back to his hometown of Sarasota, FL. What they found, was that they could only afford to live in the cheaper neighborhood of Bradenton. A cheaper, dirtier, and smelly smellier place (the place really did smell like a fart!). Not satisfied with their lot in life, the hero couple decided that leaving the United States was the best move. But where to go? Enter Costa Rica and the traveling pair!

Chapter 4

Costa Rica = Pura Vida

The power couple did it! They saved and worked, worked and saved. They sold ALL of their possessions via a garage sale, bought one-way plane tickets, and set their sights on new beginnings. His only regret, selling his Zune mp3 player. They made it! Right? October 26, 2019 was going to be the start of a very good year. That's called forshadowing...

Chapter 5

Panic @ the Pandemic!

Lockdown! Beaches Closed! Status Unknown! Time passes...quickly slowly. Eventually, things start to open. Life-Saving vaccine is made available. Hark! A light at the end of this panic tunnel. Our heros begin anew. Things improve, people survive, and life begins again. Our Heros think of the future. And Brett starts with learning a new language...
